Humany Skeleton Chat plugin

  1. Open the Bot by clicking the icon in lower right
  2. Click "Not the answer you needed?" and then "Contact us"
  3. Enter your name and click "Chat"
The dummy chat ( ./src/mock-chat-client.js) will now start emulating a real chat. Use this template to author a customized plugin for your chat service. Happy coding!
